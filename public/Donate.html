FAQs

Need quick answers?

How will my donation be used?

100% of donations go directly to programs supporting orphans, clean water projects, and community welfare initiatives.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to SAM Life Savers are tax-deductible in Pakistan. You’ll receive an official receipt for your records.

Can I donate internationally?

Absolutely. We accept secure donations from anywhere in the world via mobile wallets or direct bank transfer.

How can I get updates about my donation?

We email regular impact updates and you can reach our donor desk anytime for project-specific progress.

Can I sponsor a specific child or project?

Yes. Reach out to the team to learn about orphan sponsorships, water wells, or relief campaigns you can fully fund.

Ready to make a difference?

Your generosity can transform lives today. Fund a meal, a well, or a safe home.

Ramadan Iftar Drive: Feeding 200+ People Daily in Pakistan

Dec 05, 2025Iftar Drive

The Spirit of Giving During Ramadan

Ramadan is a time of spiritual reflection, self-improvement, and heightened devotion for Muslims around the world. In Pakistan, it is also a time when the spirit of giving reaches its peak. However, for millions of families living below the poverty line, breaking their fast with a proper meal remains a daily struggle. This is where SAM Life Savers steps in with our annual Iftar Drive.

Hunger in Pakistan: A Persistent Challenge

According to the World Food Programme, approximately 36.9% of Pakistans population faces moderate to severe food insecurity. During Ramadan, when families fast from dawn to sunset, the evening iftar meal becomes even more significant. For many underprivileged families, affording a nutritious iftar is simply not possible.

Our Iftar Drive Initiative

Every Ramadan, SAM Life Savers organizes a comprehensive Iftar Drive across Rawalpindi and Islamabad. Our dedicated team of student volunteers works tirelessly to prepare and distribute hot, nutritious iftar meals to those in need. On a daily basis, we serve over 200 people including daily wage workers and laborers, homeless individuals, orphans, elderly citizens, and hospital patients and their families.

What Your Donation Provides

With just PKR 500 (approximately $2), you can sponsor an iftar meal for one person. Each meal includes dates for breaking the fast, a refreshing drink such as Rooh Afza or lemonade, a main course like biryani or pulao, fresh fruits, and sweet treats.

The Impact of Community Support

Last Ramadan, thanks to generous donors like you, we were able to serve over 6,000 iftar meals throughout the month, engage 50+ student volunteers, create a sense of community and belonging, and spread the true spirit of Ramadan.

How You Can Help

There are many ways to contribute to our Iftar Drive. You can donate funds to sponsor iftar meals, volunteer your time to help with preparation and distribution, spread awareness on social media, or organize an iftar sponsorship in your neighborhood.

No one should go hungry during the blessed month of Ramadan. Join SAM Life Savers in our mission to feed the hungry and spread kindness. Be the light. Save a life.

We are on the mission to change the lives. Join us if you would like to be the one who is passionate to community work.
